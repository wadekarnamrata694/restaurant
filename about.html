About Mejwaani


Tradition since 1998

Mast Marathi is a Quick Serve Restaurant that serves Maharashtrian Vegetarian food, in a traditional yet quirky and lively ambience. We serve nostalgic and most loved foods handpicked from Various parts of Maharashtra. The Foods we serve are prepared in an authentic style with the blend of authentic Maharashtrian Masala which suits the palate of people from all over India. seasonal ingredients.

Our menu includes the food which is rich is taste and free of colour, additives and preservatives so that the customers can enjoy it every day. It is hygienically cooked fresh each day and nothing is deep-frozen. Hence it is light not only on the pocket but also on the tummy.
